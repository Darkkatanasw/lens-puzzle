How to Play

  1. Click Shuffle to start the puzzle.
  2. Tap or click a tile, then tap/click an adjacent tile to swap them. Tiles will slide smoothly.
  3. Some tiles are rotated. Double-click, right-click, or tap a tile to rotate it 90Â°.
  4. Arrange and rotate all tiles to restore the original image.
  5. Click Hint to briefly see the completed image.
  6. Try to solve the puzzle in as few moves and as quickly as possible!
